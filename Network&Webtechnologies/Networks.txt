LAN (Local Area Network):
- A LAN cable is used to transmit data from one computer to other devices within a local network.

Switch:
- All computers are connected to a single switch, forming a star topology.
- The layout of a network is called a topology. Local networks commonly use the star topology.

Router:
- A router is a device used to connect different networks.
- Similar to a switch with multiple ports, a router has interfaces through which other switches and routers are connected.
